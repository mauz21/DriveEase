# DriveEase Rentals - Premium Car & Professional Driver Platform

DriveEase is a luxury-focused car rental and professional chauffeur service platform. Designed with a sophisticated Gold (#FFD700) and Black aesthetic, it offers a seamless experience for renting high-end vehicles and hiring expert drivers.

## üåü Key Features

- **Exclusive Fleet**: Browse and book from a curated collection of luxury and performance vehicles.
- **Professional Chauffeurs**: Hire vetted, high-rated drivers for a premium travel experience.
- **VIP Concierge**: Personalized assistance for all your travel needs.
- **Rewards & Loyalty**: Earn points and unlock exclusive benefits with every journey.
- **Sustainability Tracker**: Monitor the environmental impact and go green with our electric fleet.
- **Luxury Design**: Glass-morphism UI with premium gold accents and smooth animations.

## üöÄ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
- **Authentication**: Replit Auth
- **Routing**: Wouter
